Mononoke the Movie: The Ashes of Rage In July 2024, it was announced that a sequel was in production, and later that month, it was announced that the Mononoke film project will have three films. The second film, titled Mononoke the Movie: The Ashes of Rage, was released on March 14, 2025. The third film is set to release in 2026.
